About this site

This site (verun-starknet-mvp.vercel.app) hosts the Verun Protocol Starknet MVP — an open-source, early-stage technical demonstration of an agent trust score layer anchored on Starknet.

⚠ BETA NOTICE
Verun Protocol is an early-stage MVP running on Starknet Sepolia testnet. The protocol, APIs, smart contracts, validator set, design, and terminology are subject to change as the protocol evolves. Addresses and transaction hashes shown are testnet-only and hold no economic value. No financial product, investment service, or regulated activity is offered through this site.
